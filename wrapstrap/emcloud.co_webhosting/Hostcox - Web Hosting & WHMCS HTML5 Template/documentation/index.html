About Hostcox

Hostcox is a fully responsive Domain & Web Hosting template with WHMCS, suited perfectly for any type of hosting business with its easy to use and customizable layout and style made by Bootstrap 4 framework.

It comes with 8 different Home page variations and many other pages as well as a matching WHMCS template so you can easily build a website in minutes and begin selling your web hosting services. It's very easy to use Just change your Text, Colors and Images to get your website ready.

Please read the how-to-use-hostcox-whmcs-matching-template.txt instrcution. you will get it in hostcox-whmcs folder.

We usa a axaj based PHP contact form in contact.html page

You just need to add your email adderss in contact.php file. Open contact.php file and replace yourid@yourdomain.com with your mail address in this line $address = "yourid@yourdomain.com";
